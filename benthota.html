Bentota Turtle Hatchery

About Us

The Bentota Turtle Hatchery is a conservation project dedicated to protecting and preserving sea turtles in Bentota, Sri Lanka. We aim to raise awareness about the threats faced by these majestic creatures and provide a safe environment for their nesting and hatching.

Turtle Conservation

Our hatchery plays a vital role in the conservation of sea turtles by collecting and incubating eggs. Once the hatchlings are ready, we release them into the ocean, increasing their chances of survival. Visitors can witness this incredible process and learn more about the different species of sea turtles found in the area.

Visit Us

We welcome visitors to the Bentota Turtle Hatchery to experience the wonder of sea turtles up close. Come and see our conservation efforts, interact with the hatchlings, and learn about the challenges they face in the wild. Your support and contributions help us continue our important work.
