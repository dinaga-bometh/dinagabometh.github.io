Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery,

The Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery, located in Kosgoda, Sri Lanka, is a conservation project dedicated to protecting and preserving sea turtles. The hatchery collects and safeguards turtle eggs, ensuring their safe incubation until they hatch. Visitors can witness the hatching process and participate in releasing the baby turtles into the ocean. The project also conducts research and educational programs to raise awareness about sea turtle conservation.

Turtle Conservation

Turtle conservation in Kosgoda involves monitoring nesting beaches, collecting and protecting turtle eggs, and promoting the survival of hatchlings. Trained conservationists and volunteers monitor nesting activities, safeguarding nests from predators and human interference. Eggs are collected and incubated in controlled environments to enhance their chances of survival. The project also focuses on raising awareness about turtle conservation among locals and tourists.
